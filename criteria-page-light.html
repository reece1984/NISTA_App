Gate Review Criteria

Assessment criteria for Gate 0: Strategic Assessment

IPA Gate 0: Strategic Assessment Review Workbook

Official guidance document with detailed evidence requirements

20 Total Criteria
5 Critical
2 Gateway Blockers
Strategic
6 criteria • 2 critical
G0-1.1 Is the strategic context for the programme clearly articulated, including the policy drivers and strategic objectives?
G0-1.2 Is the problem or opportunity that the programme addresses clearly defined with supporting evidence?
G0-1.3 Are the intended strategic outcomes clearly defined and measurable?
G0-1.4 Does the programme fit within the departmental portfolio and has it been prioritised accordingly?
G0-1.5 Is the programme aligned to Spending Review settlements and HMT expectations? Critical
G0-1.6 Has appropriate ministerial approval been obtained for programme initiation? Blocker
Economic
3 criteria
G0-2.1 Has an initial economic assessment been undertaken to establish potential value for money?
G0-2.2 Has a wide range of options been identified for further analysis, including do nothing/minimum?
G0-2.3 Have the potential benefits been identified and are they realistic?
Commercial
2 criteria
G0-3.1 Is there early understanding of the market and potential delivery routes?
G0-3.2 Is there early indication that the programme is commercially viable?
Financial
3 criteria • 1 critical
G0-4.1 Is there a reasonable basis for the initial cost estimate?
G0-4.2 Is the potential funding route identified and realistic? Critical
G0-4.3 Have key financial risks been identified at this early stage?
Management
6 criteria • 2 critical
G0-5.1 Has a suitable Senior Responsible Owner been appointed with appropriate skills and authority? Blocker
G0-5.2 Is an appropriate governance framework being established? Critical
G0-5.3 Have initial resource requirements been identified?
G0-5.4 Is there a realistic high-level timeline for the programme?
G0-5.5 Have key strategic risks been identified?
G0-5.6 Is there a plan for how the programme will be assured?
