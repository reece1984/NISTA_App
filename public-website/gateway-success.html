FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions

Q: How accurate are the predictions?
Our AI assessment engine uses the same criteria frameworks as IPA reviewers (NISTA/PAR). We've validated our predictions against official NAO and IPA reviews with high correlation. However, actual gateway outcomes depend on panel composition and interview performance.
Q: What documentation do I need to upload?
Typical documents include: Business Case (Full and Outline), Project Plans, Governance Framework, Risk Register, Benefits Management Strategy, Procurement Strategy, and Stakeholder Engagement Plans. The platform guides you through recommended documents for each gateway type.
Q: How long does an assessment take?
Initial assessment results are available within minutes of uploading your documentation. The platform provides an immediate predicted rating with detailed findings. You can then work through the prioritized action plan over the following weeks.
Q: Is my programme data secure?
Yes. All data is stored in UK South Azure region with enterprise-grade encryption. We're ISO 27001 compliant and Cyber Essentials Plus certified. Your documentation never leaves the UK and is only accessible by your authorized team members.
Q: What's included in the pilot programme?
Pilot participants receive priority access, dedicated onboarding support, direct access to our product team, preferential terms, and the opportunity to shape the product roadmap. We work closely with pilot clients to ensure success.
Q: Can Gateway Success replace my consultants?
Gateway Success builds in-house capability and dramatically reduces consultant dependency. Many clients use us alongside strategic consultancy support but eliminate the need for expensive documentation review services (typically £150K+). You maintain full control and develop institutional knowledge.
